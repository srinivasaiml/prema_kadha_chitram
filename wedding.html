Episode 01
March 18, 2021

Modati Choopu

Adhi oka sadharana roju… kaani aa roju lo oka asadharana kshanam undi…

Exam hall lo andaru paper lo busy ga unnaru… time slow ga nadusthundi…

Vasu kuda alage paper chusthunnaadu… Kaani… oka chinna choopu… oka chinna turn…

Aame ni chusina aa kshanam lo… prapancham motham nilichipoyindi…

Sound anni kalisipoyayi… gunde maathram matladindi… Aame eyes lo chusina aa velugu… aame lo unna aa shantham…

Vasu ki ardham ayyindi… Idhi just choopu kaadu… idi aarambham ❤️

Episode 02
September 29, 2021

Prema Prakatinchadam

Roju roju ki matalu perigayi… matalu lo prema kalisindi… Navvulu… raatiranta chats… chinna chinna kopamulu… Anni kalisi oka andamaina bandham ni tayaruchesayi…

Kaani oka roju… Gunde lo unna maatlu… inkaa nilavadam sadhyam kaaledu… Nervous ga… prematho… aadu cheppadu… “Nenu ninnu prema chesthunnaanu…” ❤️

Aa kshanam lo kalam malli aagipoyindi… Prathi second oka yugam laga anipinchindi… Appudu… Aame oka navvu tho… oka maatalo… “Nenu kuda…” 💖

Aa samadhanam… Vasu jeevitham lo oka kottha velugu teesukochindi.

Episode 03
October 18, 2023

Nijamaina Kalupu

Chala rojulu… phone lo matladukunnaaru… Kaani ee roju… matalu kaadu… manasulu kalisina roju…

Iddaru oka chotlo nilchunnaru… Cheppadaniki matalu levu… kani anipinche bhaavalu chala unnayi… Aame kindaki chusthundi… Aadu navvuthunnadu…

Aa madhya unna silence… Konchem konchem ga… santosham ga marindi… Endukante… Ippudu vallaki telisindi… dooram anedi shariram lo untundi… kaani manasulu eppudu kalisey untayi ❤️

Episode 04
July 1, 2024

Modati Muddu

Theatre lo movie nadusthundi… kani vallaki aa kathaa lo interest ledu… Endukante… vallaki vallaa kathaa mukhyam…

Light and darkness madhya… oka silent moment… Valla eyes kalisayi… Aa choopu lo unna prema… aa silence lo unna artham…

Mellaga… Iddaru daggara ayyru… Gunde dhadkan vinipisthundi… Mari… oka madhuramaina modati muddu 💋

Aa kshanam… chinna dhi kaani… jeevitham anthaa gurthundipoye dhi…

Episode 05
March 11, 2026

Prayanam

Train night lo nadusthundi… bayata prapancham fast ga tiruguthundi… Kaani vallaki… time slow ayyindi… Window pakkana kurchuni… kalisi aa journey ni enjoy chesthunnaaru…

Uma… mellaga Vasu chest meedha thala petti… aa heartbeat vini… Aame ki telisindi… Idi prema kaadu… idi jeevitham ❤️

Vasu kuda ardham chesukunnadu… Idi journey kaadu… idi forever prayanam…
